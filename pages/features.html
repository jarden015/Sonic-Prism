Features

• Easy access magic! Just move mouse to top left corner to turn on. Top right to turn off. Bottom row also turns off.
• Menu in top left automatically shows. (it can block clicks from what is underneath).
• Play on next click synergy magic!
• Global hotkeys so you can press play or pause or next or previous when at another program.
• Bluetooth headphone play/pause, next, previous buttons work!
• Can play songs or your transparent webm videos at the same time as any click.
• Mouse over audio samples on hover with many configurations!
• Real-time system wide sound visualizers!
