Features

• Easy access magic! Just move mouse to top left corner to turn on. Top right to turn off. Bottom row also turns off.
• Click menu in top left (it blocks clicks from what is underneath).
• Play on next click and play next on next click synergy magic!
• Global hotkeys so you can press play or pause or next or previous when at another program.
• Bluetooth headphone play/pause, next, previous, and volume buttons work!
• Can load other urls but DRM‑protected platforms like movie sites, Spotify, Apple music, Amazon music, and probably some others wont play the media. You Tube and Music You Tube work.
• Can play songs at the same time as your videos or any click.
• It may even "know" what a good song is for the part of the movie you are at (this is best used when set to "start mix" in Music You Tube so there is a pool of songs, then just click play next on next click in the menu)!
• There is a clipboard url feature that is not very obvious. If you have a valid url (start with http:// or https://, from youtube.com, youtu.be, or music.youtube.com, valid YouTube video ID) on the clipboard when you click Play On Next Click or Play Next On Next Click it will load it for the click. This can load the url that is in the share function on the ... functions on Music You Tube and also it can load You Tube pages.
• If you have any url with http in it on the clipboard when you click the URL button it will try to load that page. This stops the music at this time. I may add a second webview for this in the future.
